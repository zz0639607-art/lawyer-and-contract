Questions

Frequently asked

Everything you need to know about LexAI.

Is LexAI a real law firm? ▼
No. LexAI is an AI-powered tool for understanding contracts. We are not a law firm and do not provide licensed legal advice. For important legal decisions — lawsuits, major contracts, regulatory matters — always consult a qualified attorney.
Is my contract data private? ▼
Yes. Your contract text is sent directly to Google's Gemini API for analysis and is never stored on LexAI's servers. Google's API data usage policies apply. We strongly recommend removing personally identifiable information before uploading sensitive documents.
Which languages are supported? ▼
LexAI can analyze contracts in over 200 languages, including Chinese, Arabic, Spanish, French, Japanese, Korean, German, Hindi, Portuguese, Russian, and many more. You can also request your report in any of these languages.
How accurate is the AI analysis? ▼
LexAI uses Google Gemini, one of the world's most advanced language models. It is highly effective at identifying common risk patterns, unusual clauses, and missing protections. However, no AI is perfect — results should be a starting point, not a final legal opinion.
Do I need a Gemini API key? ▼
Yes. You need a free Google Gemini API key from aistudio.google.com — it's free and takes less than 2 minutes. Google provides a generous free tier sufficient for regular personal use.
What types of contracts can LexAI analyze? ▼
LexAI can analyze virtually any written contract: employment agreements, rental leases, NDAs, service agreements, purchase contracts, franchise agreements, loan agreements, partnership agreements, IP licensing deals, and more.
